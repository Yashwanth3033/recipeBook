Tomato Basil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups tomatoes, peeled, seeded, and chopped
  • 4 cups tomato juice
  • 14 leaves fresh basil
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. In a large saucepan, combine tomatoes and juice. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  2. Puree the tomato mixture along with the basil leaves, and return to the saucepan.
  3. Place the saucepan over medium heat, and stir in cream and butter. Continue stirring until well blended. Season with salt and pepper.

VEGETABLE SOUP

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1 carrot finely chopped
  • 1 capsicum finely chopped
  • 1 spoon chopped beans
  • 1 spoon chopped garlic
  • 1 spoon chopped ginger
  • 1 spoon butter
  • 2 spoon cournflour soaked in 1/2 cup Water
  • 1/2 spoon salt or as per taste
  • 1/4 spoon black pepper powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Heat butter in a pan and put ginger - garlic saute for 1 minutes.Then add all chopped vegies one by one and saute them for 3-4 minutes.
  2. Add 3 cup of water and soaked cournflour mix well without any lumps.Boil it for 2 -3 min on midium flame.
  3. Add salt and black pepper powder and mix well.Switch off the gas and serve hot.
