Coffee and Tea

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of ground coffee
  • 1 tea bag (black or green tea)
  • 1-2 tablespoons of sugar (optional)
  • Milk or cream (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Boil the water in a kettle. Add ground coffee to a coffee maker or a French press.
  2. Pour boiling water over the coffee grounds.
  3. Let it brew for 4-5 minutes.
  4. Pour the coffee into a cup. It becomes coffee.
  5. Let it steep for 3-5 minutes.
  6. Remove the tea bag. It becomes tea.
  7. Add sugar and milk or cream to taste.

chilli lemon soda

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup goli soda water
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice (freshly squeezed)
  • 1/2 teaspoon black salt
  • 1 teaspoon green chilli paste
  • 1/4 chat malasa
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon slices and fresh mint leaves for garnish
Instructions:
  1. In a glass, combine the lemon juice, chaat masala, black salt, red chilli powder, and sugar. Stir well to dissolve the sugar.
  2. Add ice cubes to the glass.
  3. Pour soda water over the mixture and stir gently to combine.
  4. Garnish with lemon slices and fresh mint leaves.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing Chilli Nimbu Soda!
