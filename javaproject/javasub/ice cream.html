Gadbad Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup mango ice cream
  • 1 cup strawberry ice cream
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (any available nuts like cashew and almonds)
  • 1/2 cup chopped fruits (any available fruits like apple and pineapple)
  • 1/4 cup chocolate syrup
  • 1/4 cup fruit syrup (mango, strawberry)
  • Fresh fruits for garnish (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Start by scooping a few scoops of vanilla ice cream into a bowl or serving glass.
  2. Add a few scoops of mango ice cream on top of the vanilla ice cream.
  3. Next, add scoops of strawberry ice cream on top of the mango ice cream.
  4. Drizzle chocolate syrup and fruit syrups over the ice cream scoops.
  5. Sprinkle chopped nuts on top for a crunchy texture.
  6. Garnish with fresh fruits if desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your Gadbad Ice Cream!

Nut Full chacobar Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 100g mixed nuts (e.g., almonds, hazelnuts, walnuts), roughly chopped
  • 150ml heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
Instructions:
  1. In a heatproof bowl, melt the dark chocolate over a pot of simmering water (double boiler method). Stir occasionally until smooth.
  2. In a separate saucepan, heat the heavy cream until it begins to simmer. Remove from heat and stir in the honey and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the hot cream mixture into the melted chocolate and whisk until fully combined and smooth.
  4. Add a pinch of salt to the chocolate mixture and stir in the chopped nuts.
  5. Pour the mixture into serving glasses or bowls and let it cool to room temperature. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until set.
  6. Serve chilled and enjoy your nut-filled chocolate cream!
